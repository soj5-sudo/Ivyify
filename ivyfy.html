How We Work

A three-step admissions strategy, built around you.

01 / Discover

We map your story before we write a word.

Academics, activities, the parts of your life that don't fit on a form — we spend real time finding the throughline that connects them, so everything we write afterward has somewhere to point.

02 / Craft

We build the application, sentence by sentence.

Essays, activities lists, and interview prep — drafted and redrafted in your voice, not ours. You'll never submit a sentence you wouldn't say out loud.

03 / Deliver

We tune every application to the school reading it.

What Cornell wants to see and what Princeton wants to see aren't the same thing. We finalize each application around what that specific committee actually cares about.
